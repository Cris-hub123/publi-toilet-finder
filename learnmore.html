How Our Website Helps Promote SDG 6

PubliToilet supports Sustainable Development Goal 6: Clean Water and Sanitation by improving access to clean, inclusive, and well-maintained public restrooms through the following initiatives:

Improving Access to Safe Sanitation
We help users find nearby public toilets via an interactive map, reducing open defecation and promoting proper hygiene practices.

Empowering Communities
Users can report maintenance issues and rate cleanliness, helping local governments uphold sanitation standards.

Inclusive Access for All
Filters support PWD-friendly, gender-neutral, and accessible restrooms for everyone in the community.

Hygiene Education
Educational content on hygiene, water conservation, and public health is integrated throughout the site.

SDG 6 Alignment
Directly contributes to Targets 6.1 to 6.4 including water safety, inclusivity, pollution reduction, and efficient water use.

Tech for Public Good
This prototype uses local storage, but a full version would use a robust backend to connect users with essential sanitation services in real time.
