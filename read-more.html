Did You Know?

1 in 4 people worldwide still lack access to safe drinking water

3.6 billion people lack access to adequate sanitation.

Water scarcity affects 4 billion people for at least one month every year.

7 million Filipinos still rely on unsafe sanitation facilities or have no toilet at all.

Sanitation-related diseases cause major health problems, especially during the rainy seasons.

Public Toilets in urban areas are often inadequate, leading to health risks and environmental concerns.
