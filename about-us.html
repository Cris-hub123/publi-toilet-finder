PubliToilet is a student-led project created by a team at FEU Tech’s College of Computer Studies. Our web platform helps users find nearby public toilets, rate cleanliness, report issues to local authorities, and access hygiene education. Supporting UN Sustainable Development Goal 6, we promote accessible and inclusive sanitation through interactive maps, accessibility filters, a reporting system, and educational page.

Learn More

Our Mission

To provide a user-centered digital platform that ensures safe, clean, and accessible public toilets for all by enabling real-time location services, reporting tools, and educational resources—promoting hygiene, inclusivity, and public health in alignment with Sustainable Development Goal 6: Clean Water and Sanitation.

Our Vision

To build a connected and empowered community where every individual has equal access to clean, well-maintained, and inclusive sanitation facilities—contributing to a healthier, more sustainable, and socially responsible society.

What We Do

Our service and goals.

Locate Toilets

We provide real-time location services to help you find nearby public toilets easily.

Report Issues

We enable users to report issues directly to local government units (LGUs) for development and maintenance.

Community Feedback

We empower the community to voice out for the betterment of sanitation through our rate and report system.

Promote SDG 6

Our platform actively promotes Sustainable Development Goal 6: Clean Water and Sanitation, contributing to global efforts.
